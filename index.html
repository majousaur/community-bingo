About the Community Booth

Our story begins with community, and for that we will always give back to ours.

In 2020, we organized local meet-ups within our Facebook group to trade and sell plants in an IKEA parking lot. These gatherings soon evolved into local pop-up markets all around Houston. From there, we decided to create a community event on a larger scale. Through PlantCon, we continue to break barriers and build bridges across the entire plant community.
